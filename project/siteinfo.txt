GAME DESCRIPTIONS:

1. Make your own story is a game to encourage creativity. Move characters adn objects arond the landscape to amke your own story!
2. Memory is a game that tests your memorization skills! As you flip cards try to make a match. Each match grants you points. Get as many as you can! You only have 60 seconds. 
3. Word Scramble is a game that tests your vocabulary! Choose a phrase and think of as many words as you can. Once you've entered them in and cannot think of more, hit the "get score" button to see your points!
